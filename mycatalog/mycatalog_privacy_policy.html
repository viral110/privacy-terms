Privacy Policy

MyCatalog – Digital Catalogue Maker for Businesses

MyCatalog (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the MyCatalog mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Mobile number for OTP-based user verification

b. Business Information

  • Business name
  • Business logo
  • Business address (optional)
  • Email address (optional)
  • Website URL (optional)

c. Product & Catalogue Information

  • Product images
  • Product names, descriptions, and specifications
  • Pricing details (optional and controlled by user)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users via OTP

Important: Your mobile number is used only for login and security purposes. We do not send promotional messages or make marketing calls.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data. Information may be disclosed only when required by law or to protect legal rights and platform security.

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

5. User Control

  • Stop using the app at any time
