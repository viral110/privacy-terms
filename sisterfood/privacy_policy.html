Privacy Policy for SisterFood

Last updated: 31-01-2026

SisterFood ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the SisterFood mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address

This information is collected when you sign up or log in using email or Google social login.

b. Health & Lifestyle Data (User-Provided)

  • Weight logs
  • Water intake
  • Sleep hours
  • Meditation logs
  • Daily check-ins
  • Workout and recipe progress

All health-related data is voluntarily provided by the user and used only for app functionality.

c. Device & Usage Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. Google Login

SisterFood allows users to sign in using Google. When you use Google login, we receive basic profile information such as your name and email address as permitted by Google.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide phase-wise recipes and workout plans
  • To track daily health activities and progress
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To respond to customer support requests

4. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

SisterFood uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat may process purchase-related information such as transaction status and subscription validity. We do not store your payment card or financial details.

5. Analytics (AppsFlyer)

We use AppsFlyer to understand app usage, measure performance, and improve marketing effectiveness. AppsFlyer may collect anonymized device and usage data in accordance with their privacy policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or health data to third parties. Data is shared only with trusted services such as Google, RevenueCat, and AppsFlyer for authentication, analytics, and purchase management.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

8. User Rights

  • You can update or delete your data within the app
  • You can stop using the app at any time
  • You may request account deletion by contacting us

9. Children’s Privacy

SisterFood is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website.
