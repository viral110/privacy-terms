Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12-02-2026

Your child’s privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Ocean Guardians (sister_ocean) collects, uses, and protects information.

1. Our Commitment to Children’s Privacy

Ocean Guardians is designed for children aged 6–12 and is built to comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

We do NOT require children to provide personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location to use the app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited, non-personal information necessary to operate and improve the app:

  • App usage data (completed challenges, streaks, progress)
  • Anonymous analytics data (device type, country, app performance)
  • Subscription status (via app stores)

We do NOT collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address (unless parent contacts us directly)
  • Precise location
  • Photos or camera data

3. How We Use Information

  • To track progress within the 90-day program
  • To unlock marine animals and regions
  • To improve educational content
  • To monitor app performance and stability

4. Third-Party Services

We use carefully selected third-party services:

  • Appsflyer – Used for analytics and app performance tracking. Appsflyer may collect anonymized device and usage data.
  • RevenueCat – Used to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. Payment processing is handled securely by Apple App Store or Google Play.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

All payments are processed securely through the respective app stores. We do not store credit card or payment information.

6. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect all stored information.

7. Data Retention

Anonymous usage data may be retained for analytics and app improvement purposes.

8. Parental Rights

Parents may contact us at any time to ask questions about data practices.
