Privacy Policy – SisterDog

Last updated: 31-12-2025

SisterDog (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the SisterDog mobile application (“App”).

By using SisterDog, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email address

This information is used for account creation, communication, subscription management, and support.

b. Location Information

We collect coarse (approximate) location only.

  • Used for walk predictions and safer route suggestions
  • Weather, lighting, and environmental insights

We do not collect precise background GPS location without your consent.

c. Usage & Analytics Data

We collect non-personal usage data through:

  • Firebase Analytics – app performance, crashes, and event tracking
  • AppsFlyer – marketing and campaign attribution analytics

2. In-App Purchases

SisterDog uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases.

  • Payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play
  • We do not store payment or card information
  • RevenueCat may process anonymous purchase identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered walk predictions
  • Improve app safety and performance
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Manage subscriptions
  • Comply with legal requirements

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We share limited data only with trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase – analytics and crash reporting
  • AppsFlyer – marketing analytics
  • RevenueCat – subscription management

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services or meet legal obligations. You may request data deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Children’s Privacy

SisterDog is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no system can guarantee complete security.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected by the updated date.
