Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12-02-2026

Your privacy is deeply important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how SisterDreams (sister_dreams) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized wellness experience, we may collect:

  • Dream entries (text you write)
  • Selected mood associated with dreams
  • Menstrual cycle information (cycle dates, selected phase)

We do NOT collect precise location data or sensitive identity documents.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your dream journal content remains private and is not sold or shared.

Cycle-related data and dream entries may be considered sensitive personal information. We treat this information with strict confidentiality and use it solely to provide in-app functionality.
